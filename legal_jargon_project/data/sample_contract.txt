This Lease Agreement is made on this 1st day of January, 2024, 
between Mr. X ("Lessor") and Ms. Y ("Lessee").

The Lessee agrees to pay monthly rent and shall not sublet the premises without prior written consent of the Lessor.  
The Lessor retains the right to terminate this agreement in case of default.  

The Lessee shall indemnify the Lessor against any claims arising out of negligence or misuse of the property.  

In case of dispute, the matter shall be referred to binding arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.  
The jurisdiction of courts shall be limited to the State of Karnataka.  

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior oral or written negotiations.
